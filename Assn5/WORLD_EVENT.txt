1) how to trigger the event?
To trigger the event, press a mouse-click anywhere in the game.

2) what should happen when the event is triggered?
When the event is triggered, the grass will be freezed and turned into ice. 

3) what the affected entities should do?
Some miners within the world changing event will turn into a giant baby and run faster than normal miners 
to get the Ore. The giant baby can also eat the wyverns.

4) what the new entity is and how it should behave?
When the event is triggered, the new entity wyvern will appear and can destroy blacksmith and vein.
